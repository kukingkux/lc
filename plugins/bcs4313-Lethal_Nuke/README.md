# Lethal Nuke

#### Scrap Value: 120-200 (customizable)
#### Scrap Spawn Weight: 24 (customizable)

The nuke can spawn on all moons, be careful or you might atomize your friends!

<img src="https://i.imgur.com/aT5P8TK.png" width="720" height="720"/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

It has 3 hitpoints. The item will warn you whenever it loses hp.

At 0 hp it causes a delayed explosion, at -1 the explosion is immediate.

Click below to see the explosion it causes!
[![Nuke Explosion](https://i.imgur.com/AR7tNoq.png)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BUlcX4ROAUI)


### Risk Factors

Dropping the nuke has a small chance to reduce its hp. The higher the drop height the higher the chance.

If you drop a nuke above a certain threshold it will immediately explode.

Explosions will damage and/or immediately explode the nuke.

Shovel hits will damage the nuke.

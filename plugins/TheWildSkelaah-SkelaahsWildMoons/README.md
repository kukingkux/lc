This mod compiles every single moon made by me. At this moment, it has 10 (somewhat) vanilla-friendly moons with different biomes and sizes and special mechanics to make them stand out from the rest.
Moons are presented in order of price:

## Important:
- For the most optimal experience on my moons, I'd like to recommend setting the game's Gamma / Brightness settings to default.

## Recommended Mods
### Mods with are compatible with my moons:

### Interior Mods
- [Wesley's Interiors](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/Magic_Wesley/WesleysInteriors/) (Grand Armoury, Greenhouse, Citadel, Sewers, Art Gallery etc.)
- [Generic Interiors](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/Generic_GMD/Generic_Interiors/) (Storehouse, Drains)
- DemonMae's Interiors ([ACF](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/DemonMae/ACF_Interior/), [Gothic Monastery](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/DemonMae/Gothic_Monastery_Interior/), [Subsystems](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/DemonMae/SubSystems_Interior/), [Grey Apartments](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/DemonMae/Gray_Apartments_Interior/))
- Beanie's Interiors ([Deepcore Mines](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/Beaniebe/Deepcore_Mines/), [Vehicle Hangar](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/Beaniebe/Vehicle_Hangar/))

### Other Mods
- bio-monty's [Biodiversity](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/super_fucking_cool_and_badass_team/Biodiversity/), a lot of my moons have this mod's enemy weights.
- Zigzag's [LegendWeathers](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/Zigzag/LegendWeathers/), custom compatibilty for Majora Moon with all my moons.
- [Wesley's Weathers](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/Magic_Wesley/Wesleys_Weathers/), because it's cool.

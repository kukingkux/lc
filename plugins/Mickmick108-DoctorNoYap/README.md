# Doctor No Yap


I love the doctor mod, but his speech is legit from the first time he appears... And it's long, and gets old rather quick.

## Features

- Has company alert and tune at the begining of said speeches/voice lines

- 6 short spawn voice lines, with one semi-common variant, and one super rare sub-variant (Which the rare one be toggled).

- Rare Original first encounter speech (no laugh as it cut off sadly) (Which can be toggled).

- Bell toll speech (Which can be toggled).

## bugs

- Audio made be disorted when you move your head quickly, but that is an issue with the Doctor enemy mod itself-

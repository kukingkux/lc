# The Doctor
One of the great scientifics at Playtime Co. Now reduced to brains stored and kept alive within Vital System Centers scattered across various facilities throughout the universe.

One of Poppy Playtime's main enemies, found in Chapter 4.
Adds a new "Mini-Boss" enemy very rare to find upon landing, capable of announcing its presence in your ship's speaker so that everyone knows that ***he*** is inside.

A good number of options can be changed in their respective config for customization to everyone's taste.

# Gameplay
<details>
<summary>Spoilers</summary>
This "mini-boss" is composed of multiple of his "robots", distributed throughout the interior in random locations, able to block paths or watch from certain points of the facility.<br>
<br>
These robots will remain stationary most of the time, but if they detect you through their scan, they will begin chasing you until they lose sight of you.<br>
<br>
Find the one controlling these robots; you'll need physical strength to defeat them.
<br><br>
<details>
<summary>Items</summary>
Some items can help you locate the controller:<br>
- The Eye: lets you see from the controller’s point of view — bodies and the player become visible through walls<br>
- The Heart: beats faster the closer you get to the controller
</details>

## Reward
<details>
<summary>Description</summary>

- Summons a clone that lures nearby enemies, then disappears in a cloud of smoke, dealing minor area damage
- Cooldown: 120 seconds

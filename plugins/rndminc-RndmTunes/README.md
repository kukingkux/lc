# RndmTunes
Currently this mod adds following songs in low quality to your Company Cruisers radio:
- California Gurls
- Funky Town
- Happy
- Last Friday Nigh
- Revenge - Captain Sparkles
- Billie Jean
- Party Rock Anthem
- Lady Hear Me Tonight
- Around The World - Daft Punk
- Sh boom (Life could be a dream) 
- Wake me Up before you Go Go
- Blinding Lights
- Im Still Standing
- Le Fish
- (NEW) Brother Louie - Modern Talking
- (NEW) Cheri Cheri Lady - Modern Talking
- (NEW) You're my Heart, You're my Soul - Modern Talking
- (NEW) Nightcall - Kavinsky (Something inside you - Drive Meme Song)

More songs may be added in the future. This mod should be compatible with other [CruiserTunes](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/Mellowdy/CruiserTunes/) mods/addons.

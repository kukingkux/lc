## About the mod

My Lethal Company Cosmetic mod offers a creative way to enhance your gameplay experience with (mostly) unique and event-themed cosmetics. I actively engage with the Lethal Company Modding community, taking suggestions and collaborating with streamers and YouTubers to create custom designs. Seasonal events like Christmas and Halloween are celebrated with exclusive cosmetic updates to keep the mod festive and fresh. Your ideas and feedback are always welcome as I strive to make this mod as fun and customizable as possible! If you want to give a suggestion of your own join the LCM Discord. Or find me directly in my thread (linked below).

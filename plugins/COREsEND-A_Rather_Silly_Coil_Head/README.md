## Popper's a really sweet gal, although being left in an abandoned building for ages may have done quite a bit to her psyche

An animate inanimate, comprised of party hats, a stopwatch, flowers, gloves, yarn, a globe, straws, and of course a party popper!

- Includes a version with silly sounds, one without, and a default Coilhead with the goofy sounds.  The model replacement with silly sounds is enabled by default in the skin registry.
  - Festive and spooky variations are included, but also disabled by default.

# SafeCompanyUnion

This pack aims to deliver on improvements to the base game but not detract _too_ much from its core loop. Proven to create interesting situations and a dynamic experience. Mileage will vary.

## What's included?

Currently, around 260 mods:

- Custom moons\* & interiors
- Enemies and enemy interactions
- Scrap, tools, and ship upgrades
- Weathers
- Cosmetics
- Suits
- Emotes
- Gambling
- Fixes
- Many miscellaneous adjustments and balance changes

\* This pack includes moons from [Kenji's Moons Taldor Saga](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/rectorado/Kenjis_Moons_Taldor_Saga/), which you may want to play through as a standalone beforehand!

#### Planned modpack changes

- Per-moon, assign and balance fitting interiors/enemies

## Updating the modpack

**It's always best to reinstall the pack when updating!**
Sometimes mods are removed from the pack due to incompatibility, bugs, or in preference of others. Notable changes will be mentioned in the changelog.

`Don't update mods after installing the pack.` Newer versions of mods have bugs or need configuring.

## Keybinds

Here are some useful keybinds:

- Terminal:
  - `Tab` to autocomplete
- Reserved slots:
  - Hold `Left Alt` to select
  - `Scroll` to switch slots
  - `F` to toggle flashlight (not all supported)
- Emotes:
  - TooManyEmotes:
    - Hold `` ` `` and use your mouse to select an emote
    - Hold `Left Alt` while emoting to fix your player's rotation and only pan your camera
- Spray Paint:
  - `T` to toggle colour
  - `+` & `-` to adjust size

## Terminal Commands

You can use the `simulate` command to check the chances of interiors on a moon (e.g. `simulate titan` or `simulate` to check the current moon). If the moon has a space in its name, replace it with an underscore.

## Troubleshooting

Be sure to check out the [Lethal Company Modding](https://discord.gg/XeyYqRdRGC) Discord server!

<details>
<summary><b>Some common questions/current issues</b></summary>

### I can't interact with anything or drop items!

Pressing the `,` key should fix* this. If not, then warn others not to pick up the item that caused this!
<br><br>
*Pressing this while _not_ bugged, will cause you to _become_ bugged. Simply press again to fix.

### I can only see a black foggy screen :(

**I am trying to join someone**<br>
Firstly, restart your game and try again. Otherwise, get the host to increase their `Connection timeout ms` under LobbyControl's config to the max.

**I am the host**<br>
See if it happens on a new save.

If the above don't work for you as host/client:

1. Delete the mod profile
2. Clear your mod manager's cache
3. Reinstall the modpack on a new profile

### The gambling machines state "already using a machine"

This seems to happen when using these machines with LethalCasino's chips and they become 0 value. Thus, it is best to avoid using these chips at the outside machines.

### I want to test with Imperium but the game crashes

Disable BetterSprayPaint and you're good to go.
